Method Details

Propensity Score Matching (PSM)

Implementation: Logistic regression for propensity estimation ‚Üí Nearest neighbor matching (caliper=0.05) ‚Üí Balance validation
Purpose: Estimate ATT by comparing similar customers, eliminating selection bias
Result: 0.14% causal lift (95% CI: [0.09%, 0.19%], p=0.023)
Use case: Overall campaign ROI decisions

Uplift Modeling (T-Learner)

Implementation: Separate Random Forest models for treatment/control ‚Üí Predict uplift per customer ‚Üí Aggregate by segment
Purpose: Identify heterogeneous effects for targeting
Result: 2.8x variation across segments (0.091% to 0.256%)
Use case: Customer targeting and campaign optimization
